# COMPSFI-213

Welcome to COMPSFI 213: Ethical Hacking at CCC College! I’m excited to have you join this class, where we’ll explore the dynamic and challenging field of ethical hacking and penetration testing.

This course focuses on developing your skills in identifying, exploiting, and mitigating vulnerabilities, all while adhering to ethical standards and legal frameworks. A key component of this class will be our use of HackTheBox Academy, a platform designed to provide hands-on, real-world experience in ethical hacking techniques. You will also need to sign and submit by the end of the week a legal ethical hacking course agreement form or I will drop you from this course. 

## What to Expect:

Over the semester, we’ll cover topics such as reconnaissance, exploitation, privilege escalation, and reporting. Through lectures, labs, and HackTheBox Academy exercises, you’ll gain practical experience and learn industry-relevant skills. To succeed in this course, I encourage you to:

- Engage actively with the HackTheBox platform and complete assigned challenges.
- Ask questions and collaborate with peers.
- Take advantage of office hours and additional resources when needed.

## Getting Started:

To prepare for the first class:
- Sign and submit legal agreement form by end of the week. (If I don't receive this you will be dropped from the course)
- Create an Account on HackTheBox Academy: https://academy.hackthebox.com/
- Review the course syllabus on Brightspace.
- Complete the introductory reading or video assignment (details in the syllabus).
- Ensure access to HackTheBox, Brightspace, and Cengage.

This class is designed to be challenging yet rewarding, and I’m here to support you every step of the way. If you have any questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to reach out.

Looking forward to an exciting semester of learning, collaboration, and hacking (ethically, of course)!
